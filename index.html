F.A.Q.

WHAT IS LalaStars?

LalaStars is an affiliate program, by joining it you become a partner with us. This allows you to earn money through your website. By promoting LalaBet on your sources and referring players to us - you will earn a cut out of the revenue these players generate through deposits.

HOW DO I GET STARTED?

Just use the registration form https://affiliates.lala-stars.com/partner/register and fill in all the fields. After this we will validate the information you provided and you are welcome to your company account.

HOW MUCH CAN I EARN?

We value our partners. This is why you may get up to 50% from the revenue depending on your performance! And yes, all statistics are live,, up-to-date and fully transparent.

WHEN DO I GET PAID?

We will process all payouts within the third week of each month. Please note that we have a minimum earning of €100 before we make a pay-out. If your earnings are below €100, your balance will automatically transfer to the following month.

FOR HOW LONG DO THE PLAYERS I REFERRED TO LalaStars affiliate program GENERATE MONEY FOR ME?

As long as the player plays with LalaBet, you will earn a cut of the revenue the player is generating.

HOW DO JUNGLISTARS TRACK THE PLAYERS THAT I’M SENDING TO YOU?

As long as you use the links and banners you can find in Media Gallery in your LalaStars account they are connected to you by the unique tracking link.

WHAT HAPPENS IF MY ACCOUNT SHOWS NEGATIVE NET EARNINGS?

This might happen as a player can win or lose money in the casino. However, we do not carry over negative balances from one month to another.

CAN I SPEAK TO SOMEONE?

Of course! We´re here for you. Just shoot us an e-mail via affiliates@lala-stars.com and we will get back as soon as possible.

DOES IT COST ANYTHING TO JOIN LalaStars AFFILIATE PROGRAM?

No. There is no cost of joining the affiliate program.

HOW DO YOU TRACK MY PERFORMANCE?

We use a third-party software called SoftSwiss that provides updated stats every 24 hours.

WHAT PAYMENT METHODS DO YOU SUPPORT?
